Getting to the Wedding

Spokane is located in Eastern Washington State, USA and boasts a very comfortable international airport (GEG). If you are travelling from Canada, it is often cheaper to depart from a US airport. By car Spokane is a 4.5 hour drive from Seattle along I-90.
