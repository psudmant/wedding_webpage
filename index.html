Getting to the Wedding




Spokane is located in Eastern Washington State, USA and boasts a very comfortable international airport (GEG). If you are travelling from Canada, it is often cheaper to depart from a US airport. By car Spokane is a 4.5 hour drive from Seattle along I-90.

Sites


  • A

    The Glover Mansion- the wedding and reception venue

  • B

    The Davenport Hotel- A historic hotel built in 1914 that was renovated about 10 years ago. This is a 5 minute cab ride to the Glover mansion

  • C

    Spokane Club Hotel- This is a few minutes by car to the Glover mansion.

  • D

    Madison Inn- This is within walking distance of the mansion

  • E

    Barrister Winery- A local winery open during the day as a tasting room

  • F

    Wild Sage Bistro- An upscale restaurant in downtown Spokane. They have great mixed drinks!

  • G

    Mizuna- another restaurant in downtown Spokane with good lunch food and lots of vegetarian options

  • H

    Auntie's Bookstore- A beloved local bookstore. It has a casual restaurant/coffee shop (Sante) attached as well

  • I

    Riverfront Park- the site of the 1974 World's Fair, this is a large park spanning the Spokane river and Spokane falls. Includes a historic carousel, a bridge and gondola over the falls, and lots of open space

  • J

    Central Food- a nice restaurant overlooking the river and downtown Spokane

  • K

    No-Li Brewery- a local brewery with good pub food. It has a nice patio outside overlooking the river

  • L

    Italia Trattoria- An Italian restaurant in Browne's Addition, a neighborhood just west of downtown. They have a great brunch!

  • M

    The Elk- Directly across from Italia Trattoria, a local brewpub with good food and a nice outdoor patio.

  • N

    NW Museum (MAC)- local museum with art and history exhibits

  • O

    Manito Park- a large park near where Caitlin grew up, with a duck pond, formal gardens, a rose garden, and a Japanese garden, and many maple trees. Caitlin's favorite place in Spokane.

  • P

    Rockwood Bakery- a block or so from Manito park, this is a great local coffeeshop with great pastries and breakfast or lunch food.

  • Q

    Manito Tap House- A newer restaurant that features tons of beers on tap and good burgers.

  • R

    South Perry Pizza- A casual pizza place located in the South Perry neighborhood (really just a street), which also has a great farmer's market on Sundays.

  • S

    Donut Parade-

You could also check out AirBnB for accomodations. You would want to focus on The South Hill or Browne's Addition neighborhoods.
